Symptoms

PTSD

  • Hard memories

    • Like if you ever get flashbacks of the horrifying experience that you encountered or having nightmares, feelings of distress. All of these are examples that could lead to someone feeling to get PTSD

  • Avoiding

    • When a person does not want to talk about something tragic like wars and terrorist attacks because it will fuel their distress. They tend to avoid area that smbolizes a certain event.

  • Hard changes in your mental state

    • When a person thinks that they lost all hope, become less positive about themselves, or when they are less interested in the topic they really enjoy. All of these negative ideas and actions can also make them getting PTSD

  • Changes in their reactions

    • When they are very frightened, overwhelmed, and shamed about themselves. Also, they tend to having trouble focusing and have enough sleep. They also may have mood swings and that can lead to PTSD

Suicide

    • When they say like:"I wish I wasn't born" or "Why am I not dead yet? you know phrases that could signify death or suicide."
