What is OCD?

Ok, that is enough for now, but now let us get going with learning about OCD!


OCD or Obsessive-Compilsive Disorder is a type of disorder that occurs when a person has uncontrollable behaviors or actions that makes a person do it again for many times if they desire to


About 1 in 100 adults in the United States gets OCD

Causes

The main causes for OCD is that:

  • Inheritance/Genetics

    • It is pretty common for kids to expeirence it if their parents had OCD or somewhere in their family tree.

  • The Environment mentally not physically

    • If you have a tough childhood chances are that you wll somewhat face OCD or any other disorders. Mainly because of how traumatizing a certain event could be for the child to face or unserstood.

  • Brain Function

    • Sometime parts of the Brainuch as the frontal cortex and some other part of the brain and the connections aren't understood.

Symptoms

OCD has symptoms such as:

  • Obssesions

    • They are the random or thoughts that cn cause a person to suffer anxiety and it could trigger new fears, trying to make everything like clean or perfect, and beign aggressive to yourself and someone

  • Compulsions

    • That means after they have their obssesions, then they perform actions to help ease their obsessions or as in response such as doing things excessively and repeating it.

Treatment or Cures

The treatments of OCD are:

  • Medication

    • Medicines such as SRI or this (Serotonin reuptake inhibitors) are used to reuce the symptoms and as always talk with your docotrs about your prescription of these medication

  • Therapy

    • As always there are therapies such as Psychotherapy and even more. With one of them being, EX/RP (Exposure and Response Prevention) and if you don't know what that is then let me tell you, it is use to spend time with patients in a environment that triggers their OCD and helps ther to reduce them.
