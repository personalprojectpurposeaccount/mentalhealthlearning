Depression

Yes, I know that some people may know this but, it is a common type because about 1 in every 6 people will get depression in some point in their lives and it is important to know about the causes, symptoms, and treatments about it so that you will get a better understanding of the topic.


The genereal definition of depression is the emotional or mental state where you feel sadness, low self-esteem, lack of enjoyment, and ect.

Causes

It is important for you to know what can cause it so that you will have a better chance of having being treated in the early stages

  • Tragic events or Negative Change in your life

    • So, depression is can be mainly caused by some tragic event that impacted you and an example would be if one of your loved family members passed away. And, I know it can be traumatizing and it can lead you into feeling depressed


  • Family History

    • Another causes could be if one of your family members had depression before. Because if they had depression before
    it is possible that you can be depressed in some part of your life due to genetics.


  • Other diseases

    • It can also caused by other diseases as well because depression can be a symptom of some of the majors such as,
    Heart disease, Diabetes, Cancer, etc.

Symptoms

Now, if you or someone you know have some of these symptoms you should seek help by:

  • Asking a trusted adult

    • I know that some people are afraid of seeking help because they may think that it is not a big deal for the person who is actually dealing with depression but it is important that you can ask for help from your parents, teachers, guidance counselors, relatives, etc. for any help because most of the time they will always find a way to help you at any needs.

  • Calling 988 or 911 if you are thinking about suicide

But, in order to seek help you should focus on some symptoms if you or someone is having depression

  • Trouble concentrating
  • Loss of interest
  • Thinking about ending your life
  • Changes in routine
  • The feeling of sadness, irritabilty, lossing hope

And, there is more that the one's that were listed
